Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian Sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato Sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • Parsley, Dried Baisc Salt & Italian Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • 1 egg
    1. Make the meat sauce. using the tomato inredients, meats and spices
    2. Cook the noodles
    3. Grate and mix all the cheeses together
    4. Layer the lasagna pasta alternating by meat mixture and cheese mixture
    5. Cover with Foil and bake at 340 Farenhait
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
